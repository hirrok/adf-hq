# Build Brief — 2026-06-12

## Archetype: Association & Community (Class C — Strategic)
**Niche: Agricultural & Fishing Multipurpose Cooperative (Savings, Loans, Produce Marketing)**

### Why this niche, why today
Phase 2 queue order: Trades Contractor -> Professional Services -> Association & Community.
Both prior slots are COMPLETE (IronCrest Works / trades-contractor; Sterling Ledger Co. /
accounting-bookkeeping). This brief opens the final Phase 2 slot: Association & Community.

Class C purpose is Network Access, Market Influence, Trust Expansion - not direct retainer
revenue. A Multi-Purpose Cooperative (MPC) is the cleanest fit from the doctrine's own
"Cooperatives" example, is extremely PH-market-dense (CDA-regulated, present in nearly every
municipality), and Aurora Province has an active cooperative sector under its Provincial
Cooperatives Office. An MPC demo also doubles as a template for Chamber-of-Commerce / association
variants later (shared member-portal pattern).

Strategic value: an MPC build opens doors to the Provincial Cooperatives Office and CDA-registered
co-ops across Region III - a single strong reference here can cascade into multiple Class B/C
referrals (members of a co-op are themselves SMB owners - direct pipeline into Foundry's core
Class B base).

## Recommended Page Sections / Nav Structure
- **Home** - hero (mission: "Sama-samang pag-unlad" / collective growth for farmers, fisherfolk
  and small entrepreneurs), trust bar (CDA registration no., years operating, # of members,
  total assets/savings - public co-op transparency norm)
- **About / Who We Are** - history, vision-mission, Board of Directors, CDA compliance badges
- **Membership** - how to join, requirements, share capital, member benefits; "Apply for
  Membership" lead form (Layer 2)
- **Services** - Savings & Time Deposits, Loans (salary, agri-production, emergency, livelihood),
  Produce Marketing/Consolidation (buy farmers' harvest at fair price, sell in bulk), Agri-Supply
  Store (seeds, fertilizer at member discount), Insurance/Mortuary Aid (CMBA-style)
- **Programs & Advocacy** (Layer 4 - Growth) - training/seminars, youth & women's co-op programs,
  community outreach - reinforces trust/network narrative for Class C
- **News & Announcements** - General Assembly notices, dividend declarations, harvest season
  bulletins (simple CMS-style content blocks; SEO content engine)
- **Contact / Visit Us** - branch locations, office hours, contact form
- **Member Portal (Operator)** - gated link to operator.html: member account dashboard
  (savings balance, loan status/amortization schedule, dividend history), staff-side admin view
  (Layer 3 Operations + Layer 5 Intelligence - total deposits, loan portfolio health, membership
  growth)

## Brand Direction
- **Palette:** Cooperative green (#2F6B3C) + earthy gold/harvest (#C9A227) + cream/off-white
  background (#FAF7EF) + deep brown accent (#4A3728) - evokes agriculture, trust, community
  (distinct from Sterling Ledger's navy/gold and IronCrest's industrial palette)
- **Fonts:** Headings - "Merriweather" or "Lora" (warm, civic-institutional serif); Body/UI -
  "Open Sans" or "Source Sans 3" (highly legible, accessible - co-op membership skews
  older/rural)
- **Tone:** Communal, transparent, dignified - "your cooperative, your growth." Plain Filipino/
  English mix in headlines is appropriate (co-ops lean bilingual). Emphasize transparency
  (published financials, elected leadership) over sales pressure.
