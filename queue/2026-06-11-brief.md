## Brand Direction
- **Palette:** Deep navy (#1B2A4A) + slate gray (#4A5568) + accent gold/amber (#D4A24E) for
  trust + precision; off-white background (#F7F5F0)
- **Fonts:** Headings — "Fraunces" or "Playfair Display" (editorial, trustworthy serif);
  Body/UI — "Inter" or "Work Sans" (clean, modern sans)
- **Tone:** Precise, calm, reassuring authority — "we handle the numbers so you can run the
  business." Avoid corporate-cold; warm professionalism, plain-language explanations of
  BIR/tax jargon.
