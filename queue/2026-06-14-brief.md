## Recommended Page Sections / Nav Structure
- **Home** — hero: split CTA framing ("Residential" / "Commercial") above the fold, region
  served (San Luis & Aurora Province), emergency contact prominent (mobile-first, tap-to-call)
- **Services** — segmented grid: Residential (rewiring, panel upgrades, outlet/lighting
  installation, generator hookup, ceiling fans, home safety inspection) vs. Commercial
  (electrical fit-outs, maintenance contracts, PEC compliance inspections, signage/lighting,
  surge protection)
- **Emergency Service** — dedicated CTA block/page: 24/7 outage response, what counts as an
  emergency, response-time promise — this is the highest-intent search category for electricians
- **Projects / Gallery** — before/after panel upgrades, commercial installs (reinforces "real
  work, real photos" trust signal from research)
- **About / Licensing** — PEC-licensed electricians, years operating, safety certifications
- **Service Area** — San Luis + Aurora Province map/list (local SEO anchor page)
- **Contact / Get a Quote** — lead form (Layer 2), routes to operator inbox
- **Operator Portal (gated, `<archetype>2026` convention)** — Job/Ticket Tracking (residential
  vs. commercial queue, emergency flag), Maintenance Contract tracker (recurring commercial
  clients — MRR signal), Business Intelligence (jobs by category, avg response time, revenue by
  service line), Marketing Generator (seasonal templates: brownout-season emergency push,
  PEC inspection reminders for commercial clients, panel-upgrade promos), AI Console (diagnostics:
  overdue maintenance contracts, emergency response SLA breaches, top revenue service lines)

## Brand Direction
- **Palette:** Electric blue (#1E5AA8) + safety yellow/amber accent (#F2B705) + charcoal
  (#2B2B2E) + white — conveys technical precision + safety/caution without looking like a
  warning sign. Distinct from IronCrest (industrial orange/steel) and Sterling Ledger
  (navy/gold).
- **Fonts:** Headings — "Barlow Semi Condensed" or "Oswald" (technical, industrial-modern,
  good for trade-service headlines); Body/UI — "Inter" or "Source Sans 3" (clean, highly legible
  for service lists and forms).
- **Tone:** Direct, reliable, safety-first. "Power you can trust" framing. Short sentences,
  strong CTAs, emphasis on licensing/compliance for commercial credibility and fast response for
  residential urgency.
