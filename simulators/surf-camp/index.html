Getting to Baler

Baler is approximately 4 hours by bus or car from Manila via the NLEX-SCTEX-TPLEX and Baler-San Jose Road. Genesis Transport and other bus lines operate regular Manila-Baler routes from the Cubao terminal.

From Cabanatuan to Alon

Complimentary airport transfer for all Surf & Stay and Immersion guests. For Day Tripper and dorm bookings, the van ride is 45 minutes and costs ₱200–₱300. We can also arrange pick-up: just message us.

From Baler Town Center

10-minute tricycle ride from the town center. Tell your driver "Alon Surf Camp" along Sabang Beach Road. Or call ahead and we'll send someone.
